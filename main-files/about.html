About SpeedPulse

Our Mission

SpeedPulse was created with a single goal in mind: to provide the most beautiful, seamless, and privacy-respecting internet speed test experience on the web. We believe that checking your connection shouldn't require downloading heavy desktop apps or navigating through cluttered, ad-filled websites.

The Technology

Built entirely on modern, client-side web technologies (Vanilla JavaScript, HTML5, and CSS3), SpeedPulse operates directly in your browser. By utilizing global CDN endpoints from Cloudflare and IPAPI, we deliver accurate metrics for Download, Upload, Ping, and Jitter without routing your data through proprietary servers.

The Creator

SpeedPulse is proudly designed and developed by Sanskar Yadav. Passionate about Advanced Web Development, Sanskar focuses on building highly interactive, "glassmorphism" styled UI/UX applications that push the boundaries of what the browser can do.
